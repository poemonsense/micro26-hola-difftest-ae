## Limitations and reproducibility risks

- The Docker base is referenced by a mutable tag rather than an image digest,
  and apt packages are not version-pinned. A future environment refresh could
  therefore change tool versions.
- The paper comparison constants are embedded in the submitted report
  generator. The generated reports mitigate this by exposing the measured
  counters, formulas, and raw-result provenance for direct review.
- The paper manuscript is not included in this checkout. Paper values were
  checked against the explicit transcriptions in the artifact appendix and
  generated evaluation reports.

These are provenance and long-term robustness caveats; no result mismatch was
observed in the generated evaluation reports.
